abstract: >-
  We introduce ACT (Adaptive Activation Steering), a tuning-free method that adaptively shifts
  LLM activations toward the "truthful" direction at inference time, addressing diverse
  hallucination categories and improving truthfulness significantly across LLaMA, Alpaca, Vicuna
  and other models.
